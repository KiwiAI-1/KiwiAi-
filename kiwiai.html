KiwiAI Privacy Policy

KiwiAI ("we", "us", or "our") provides an AI-powered assistant designed to help students with Sparx Maths, Sparx Science, Sparx Reader, Seneca, and languagenut tasks. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Name, email address, and password.

Usage Information: Questions submitted, subject area selected, app preferences, and general usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to create and manage your account, provide AI-generated assistance, send account-related emails, improve your experience, and maintain the security of our service.

3. How We Store Your Information

Account details and preferences may be stored locally on your device and/or on secure servers. We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party providers (such as email delivery and hosting services) solely to operate KiwiAI.

5. Children's Privacy

KiwiAI may be used by students, including those under 13. We encourage parents and guardians to be involved in their child's use of KiwiAI.

6. Your Choices

You may update your username and password at any time in Settings, or request deletion of your account by contacting us.

7. Academic Integrity and Permitted Use

KiwiAI is intended to support learning. Each KiwiAI account is provided strictly for the personal use of the individual who created it. You may not access, use, or operate KiwiAI on behalf of another person, or use another person's account, without that person's express knowledge and consent. Using KiwiAI to complete work on behalf of another individual without their authorisation is strictly prohibited and constitutes a violation of this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to suspend or terminate any account found to be in breach of this provision.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, with changes reflected by an updated date above.

9. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at supportkiwi@gmail.com
